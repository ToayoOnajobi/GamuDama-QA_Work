# Bug ID: 
Title: Scene lighting overexposed upon beginning the level 

Environment: 
- Platform: Windows & macOs
- Resolution: 1920x1080 

Severity: High
Priority: High 
Frequency: Consistent 

Steps to Reproduce: 
1. Launch the game. 
2. Complete Level 1 to 7
3. Transition to Level 7. 
4. Observe environment lighting. 

Expected Result: 
- Scene lighting is expected to be bright to represent environmental changes, in this case a snow and moist environment prompted brighter lighting. 

Actual Result: 
- Scene lighting is overexposed, proves to be difficult for the player to keep track of their character during movement such as walking and shooting.
